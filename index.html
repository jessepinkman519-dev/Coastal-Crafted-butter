30-day launch path

Move clean. Move legal. Move fast.

This demo is built to sell the vision while keeping the real launch disciplined: compliance first, batch discipline second, sales testing third.

Phase 1

Compliance checkpoint

Confirm production, storage, refrigeration, labeling, and allowed sales channel before public sale.

Phase 2

Finalize labels + QR

Lock jar size, front label, ingredient/allergen panel, QR page, and scan test from printed label.

Phase 3

Micro-batch test

Produce a controlled test batch, gather feedback, identify top flavor, and refine price/package.

Phase 4

Retail / restaurant drops

Approach 3-5 aligned locations with samples, QR demo, sell sheet, and simple wholesale terms.

Compliance gate

No skipped steps.

Because this is a refrigerated dairy-based product, the brand should treat licensing, approved production area, refrigeration, ingredient labeling, allergen disclosure, and Nutrition Facts/exemption review as a launch requirement—not a detail to figure out later.

This demo is a sales and brand experience. It is not legal, food-safety, or licensing approval.
